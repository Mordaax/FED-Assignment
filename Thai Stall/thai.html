About Us

The Thai Bistro, established in 1932, is a world-renowned restaurant serving delicacies from Thailand, using ingredients native to the country. We take pride in our award, clinching 14 michelin stars since our first restaurant.

Today, we serve the heart of Thailand to over 56 countries, with 1932 bistros worldwide! We also open smaller branches within food courts and educational institutes.

No matter where you are, we can bring you closer to Thailand like never before! Come down to our bistro - you will never be disappointed!
