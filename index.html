MUNCH

About Us

Munch houses an army of stores who have been serving mouthwatering dishes for decades. The beloved hawker centre boasts a wide variety of stalls and is the place to head to for excellent local cuisine, making it one of the busiest spots during lunch and dinner hours. Stalls include international cuisines such as Thai, Western, Japanese as well as Chicken Rice and Dessert stalls.
